About

We believe in writing as the process of translating thinking into language. Our competition distills the raw essence of thinking through a time limit; our rubric rewards risk-taking, nuance, and originality. We publish the winning essay each cycle to showcase both the exemplary craft and the evolution of the competition.

Mission

To cultivate individual voice through refinement, emphasizing originality, depth, and nuance.

Vision

To deepen the pursuit of excellence, challenge the conventional status quo of essay competitions, and grow into a community of individuals diverse in thought.

Rubric Evolution

We iterate with transparency; our rubric serves to empower individual voice.

View Latest Rubric (PDF)
2025 • Quarter 1
Foundations
Established six core criteria: Clarity & Flow, Depth of Interpretation, Responsiveness to the Prompt, Profound/Interesting Ideas, Risk-Taking, Scope. This provides a holistic baseline and gives writers a clear sense of direction.
2025 • Quarter 2
Responsiveness to the Prompt
Emphasized ability for writers to challenge or even critique the prompt, which prevents the previous penalization of risk-taking. Encourages boldness and experimentation.
2025 • Quarter 3
Clarity & Flow
Defined “intentional complexity” vs. “obfuscation.” Added guidance on paragraph rhythm and transitions, rewarding not only ideas but the artistry and coherence of their delivery.
2025 • Update
Granularity
Increased rubric ceiling, changing 3 strands to 5, to emphasize nuance in marking. Allows tied, max-marks essays to be distinguished.
